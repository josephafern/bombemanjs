Instructions

What you can do:

  • Move using arrow keys (up, down, left, right)
  • Drop one bomb at a time using the spacebar
  • Break red bricks with bombs

What you can't do:

  • Move through gray bricks or bombs
  • Move through red bricks before destroying them
  • Survive a bomb blast

Objective:

  • Break bricks to access your opponent
  • Place bombs close to your opponent
  • Eliminate your opponent to win
